About DINE-INTEL

In June 2025, a Bengaluru-based food-tech startup faced severe food safety issues and delivery outages, resulting in a sharp drop in customer trust, orders, and partner engagement. DINE-INTEL was created to help such businesses recover using data-driven insights.

Our platform helps identify recoverable customer segments, track order and behavior patterns across pre-crisis, crisis, and recovery phases, evaluate delivery performance and SLA gaps, and recommend effective marketing campaigns and high-value partnerships.
